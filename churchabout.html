Our History

What originally started as a Methodist church in the early 1950's, Flint Hill Community Baptist Church quickly found it's roots and continues to grow in the small county of Powhatan, VA.

We believe the three most important things that the Gospels teach us.
That Christ died, was risen, and will come again for us someday!
