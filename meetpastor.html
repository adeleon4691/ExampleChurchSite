Meet the Pastor

Pastor John Stevens was born and raised in Raleigh, North Carolina. Pastor John fell in love with the church at a very young age. He enjoyed going to Sunday School, service as well as the youth activities throughout his whole childhood. At the age of 10, Pastor John gave his life to Christ. When Pastor John was a Senior in High School, he started to feel a call towards ministry. After receiving his acceptance letter to Liberty University in Lynchburg, VA, Pastor John started his studies in Christian Leadership & Church ministry with a concentration in Biblical Studies as well as a minor in Youth Ministries. After graduating from college, Pastor John stayed in the Lynchburg area at a church near Liberty University and started as a Youth Minister. A couple of years went by and Pastor John felt called to Chesterfield, VA where a local church was looking for an associate pastor. During his time there he continued leading youth studies as well as diving more into young adult studies, mostly connecting with the college age students in the church. When he heard about the opening for Senior Pastor at Flint Hill Community Baptist Church, Pastor John quickly jumped at the opportunity and has been leading our church here ever since.
